Chicken Strogonnof

Description

This Chicken Strogonoff is a delicious and creamy dish that is sure to become a favorite.

Made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich and flavorful sauce, this recipe is perfect for any occasion.

Serve it with white rice and crispy potato sticks for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pounds (900g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (240ml) tomato sauce or passata
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240g) sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) brandy or cognac (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (optional, for thickening)
  • 1/4 cup (60g) chopped parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Stir in the Dijon mustard, ketchup, and Worcestershire sauce.
  7. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they start to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  8. Pour in the brandy or cognac, if using, and cook until the alcohol evaporates, about 1-2 minutes.
  9. Add the tomato sauce, chicken broth, and heavy cream, stirring to combine.
  10. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the mixture simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
  11. If the sauce is too thin, stir in the cornstarch mixed with water to thicken it to your desired consistency.
  12. Stir in the butter until melted and well combined.
  13. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
  14. Serve the chicken strogonoff hot, garnished with chopped parsley. Accompany with white rice and a generous serving of potato sticks on the side.

Enjoy your wonderful Chicken Strogonnof!!
